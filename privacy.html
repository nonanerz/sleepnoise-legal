Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

SleepNoise ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

SleepNoise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app settings, saved mixes, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase data. See their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Apple processes payments and may collect data per their privacy policy at apple.com/privacy.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or location information.

Children's Privacy

SleepNoise does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
